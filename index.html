Flexible Business Models

Fixed-Bid

  • The Fixed-bid model is for projects with predetermined deliverables, accurate budgets and specified timelines.

  • Compared to other models, it is for projects with high-end budgets and predictable end-to-end solutions offering limited flexibility in project undertaking.

  • The project development stages and end goals requires minimum attention, management and direction.

Time & Material

  • Time & material is categorized as a flexible engagement model for projects with undefined requirements, large scope and extended time frames.

  • With unpredictable use of time & resources, it is an economical option for huge projects that drive for months to years.

  • This model allows great control to the clients with continuously changing requirements, goals and probabilities.

Dedicated Team

  • Expand the in-house IT team by delegating project requirements to ramp up the product development process.

  • Convenient and cost-effective model to quickly scale the team aligned to your project needs.

  • Optimal for projects with dynamic scope offering great flexibility, predictable budget and a great degree of control across the development stages.

Outstaffing

  • Outstaffing is associated with on-demand team augmentation from developers to team leads and delivery managers leveraging business-specific project needs.

  • It is quite reasonable in the budget for clients looking for a faster and more effective way to hire talents offshore without worrying about the risk of quality project deliverables.

  • This model offers complete control to the clients throughout the project commencement and delivers client-centric and favorable solutions.
